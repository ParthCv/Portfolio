Project information

Designed and developed a survival horror game like Theif with Unity, focusing on stealth mechanics. Responsible for creating the enemy AI, and implementing complex behaviour patterns to enhance gameplay challenges. Engineered sound and light detection features add depth to gameplay and enhance player immersion.
